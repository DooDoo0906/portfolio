ETour-Project

A website allows people to view, book a specific tour.I and my team build this website in order to help people save time for booking tickets (this is also our project for assignments at school).

Users can also view their profile as well as their booking history. If there is something wrong, they can edit the information immediately.

Users are free to ask questions, our staffs will answers all the questions as quick as we can.
